My top 3 movies of all-time

Hackers (1995)

A cult classic that dives into the world of computer hacking and cyber culture,
it captures the excitement of tech innovation and community.

Ex Machina (2014)

A thought-provoking exploration of artificial intelligence, this film raises questions
about technology's impact on humanity, offering both aesthetic and philosophical inspiration.

The Dark Knight (2008)

A gripping superhero film that delves into chaos and morality.
